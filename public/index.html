    How to Chat
    • Enter a Room ID to join an existing chat room, or leave it blank and click "Create New Room" to start a new one.
    • Optionally, enter your name (otherwise you'll be assigned a random guest name).
    • Share your Room ID or use the "Share Room" button in the menu to invite others.
    • Type messages in the chat box and press Enter or click Send.
    • Click "Leave Room" to exit the chat at any time.
